Tour Overview

"An exceptional cultural and historical journey, rich in encounters and panoramas, following the ancient trade exchanges between East and West."

This tour takes you to the heart of the Silk Road, from Lanzhou to Kashgar, discovering the great Buddhist, Tibetan, and Uyghur civilizations of Western China. It combines major sites such as the Maiji Caves, Bingling Si, Mogao, and Bezeklik, Tibetan monasteries, desert oases, and remnants of ancient caravan cities. The spectacular landscapes alternate between mountains, high-altitude lakes, desert dunes, and valleys of the Yellow River.
