Tour Overview

"A journey to the heart of traditional China, between ancient imperial cities, preserved villages and minority cultures."

Spectacular landscapes, from the rice terraces of Yuanyang to the stone forests of Shilin, passing through the Tibetan plateaus, punctuated by authentic encounters with the Hani, Yi, Bai, Naxi and Tibetan peoples. A unique cultural journey on the Tea and Horse Caravan Road, discovering monasteries, temples and local markets, blending nature, spirituality, craftsmanship and the legendary atmospheres of Shangri-La and Lijiang.
