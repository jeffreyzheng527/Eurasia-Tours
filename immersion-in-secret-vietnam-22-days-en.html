Tour Overview

"Between Southern Shores and Northern Rice Terraces Trekking."

Set off to discover Vietnam from South to North, between dynamic cities, floating markets, and emblematic historical sites. Explore the Mekong Delta, Hoi An, Hue, and Hanoi while immersing yourself in the daily life of local communities. In the North, hike through rice terraces, karst mountains, and traditional villages of the Hmong, Dao, and Tay ethnic groups, with homestays and participation in local activities. Conclude your journey with an unforgettable cruise on Halong Bay, amidst spectacular landscapes and authentic moments.
