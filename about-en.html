About Us

History

Eurasia Tours History

Eurasia Tours was established in 2000 and is one of the professional travel agencies designated by the French Tourism Board (Atout France) to receive global tourists. We hold an authorized license from the French Tourism Board and are insured by GROUPAMA (Insurance No. N'AA145293), possessing the French National Tourism License (IM075120157).

Leveraging our expertise in European tourism, we fully utilize the rich travel resources of China and Europe to provide exceptional travel experiences. We offer high-end, personalized travel services year-round, including European tours, business travel, study tours, cruises, and international flight bookings.

Our Mission

Eurasia Tours is committed to provide unforgettable travel experiences across Europe, North America, Asia, and Africa for Chinese tourists and global travelers alike. While maintaining our traditional strengths in official conferences, exhibitions, business visits, and economic cooperation, we continuously respond to market demands by launching innovative and diverse service projects.

Featured Programs:

  • Seasonal Camps
  • Self-improvement Retreats
  • European Self-drive Tours

Recognition

Awards & Achievements

Eurasia Tours has been recognized by the Petit Futé Guide (established in 1976, the Michelin equivalent for tourism) for many years. As a member of the Association of Tourism Professionals (APST), we are supported and encouraged by industry professionals and clients alike.

Diverse Services

We organize regular and special group tours for French tourists to Asian destinations like Japan, Vietnam, Cambodia, and Uzbekistan. We focus on culture, business, cycling, hiking, and student exchange programs. Our China routes include icons like Beijing and Shanghai, as well as in-depth tours of Yunnan and the Silk Road.

Strategic Expansion

In 2024, Eurasia Tours received strategic investment from GTT International Travel Group, becoming its first European subsidiary. Founded in 1984 in Dallas, Texas, GTT operates over 45 branches in major US cities with 600+ professionals. As North America's largest wholesale consolidator, GTT holds 35% market share with annual sales exceeding $2 billion.

US Market Growth

With our strategic partnership with GTT International, Eurasia Tours is well-positioned for expansion in the US market. Leveraging GTT's extensive network, we aim to offer exceptional travel services to a broader North American audience, connecting more travelers with our unique experiences and enhancing our global influence.

Global Services

We provide comprehensive services for both outbound and inbound tourists. Explore destinations across Asia, North America, Europe, and North Africa through our bespoke sightseeing and business tours. Starting late 2024, we will launch online booking for flights and cruises, further enhancing our seamless global travel solutions.

Trusted Partners

Since inception, Eurasia Tours has built a network of partners across hotels, ticketing, cruises, and transportation. These partnerships allow us to offer high-quality accommodation, reliable ticketing, excellent cruise lines, and convenient car rentals, ensuring every traveler's journey is seamless and enjoyable.
