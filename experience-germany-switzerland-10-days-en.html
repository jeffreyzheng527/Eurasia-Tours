Highlights

"A journey into the heart of Europe, from German historic castles to Swiss alpine lakes, experiencing the ultimate charm of two nations."

Experience the fairytale-like Neuschwanstein Castle and feel the King's extravagant dream; stroll through the old towns of Zurich and Lucerne; and take the GoldenPass Line through the Alps.
