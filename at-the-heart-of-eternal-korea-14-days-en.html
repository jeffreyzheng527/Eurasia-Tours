Tour Overview

"At the Heart of Eternal Korea" is an invitation to travel through the ages, from the Joseon Dynasty to the modernity of Seoul. This 14-day journey reveals cultural treasures, sacred mountain landscapes, and the gastronomic richness of the peninsula.

From the bustle of Seoul to the millennial temples of Gyeongju, passing through traditional Hanok villages and the coastal landscapes of Busan, this tour offers total immersion. Participate in authentic workshops like kimchi making or the tea ceremony, and discover the deep history that shaped this fascinating country.
