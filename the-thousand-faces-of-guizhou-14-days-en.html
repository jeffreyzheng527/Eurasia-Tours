Tour Overview

"A journey into the heart of southwest China, between spectacular metropolises, exceptional natural landscapes, and preserved minority cultures."

From Chongqing to the villages of the Miao, Dong, Shui, and Zhuang ethnic groups, this circuit offers authentic encounters, ancestral traditions, the Longji Rice Terraces, and the karst landscapes of Libo. It concludes between the iconic scenery of Yangshuo and Guilin, before the dazzling modernity of Shenzhen, illustrating the contrast between traditional and contemporary China.
